Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 5, 2026

Fastcircle (com.fastcircle.app) is built privacy-first. We do not collect, transmit, sell, or share any personal data.

Data We Collect

None. The App has no account system and no analytics or tracking SDKs. Any content you create stays on your device.

Device Permissions

If the App asks for a permission (such as microphone, location, HealthKit, camera, motion), it is used solely to provide the feature you requested, entirely on your device. That data is never uploaded to us or to any third party.

Third Parties

The App contains no third-party advertising, analytics, or tracking SDKs. It makes no network calls to our servers, because we operate none.

Children

The App does not knowingly collect data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

We may update this policy; changes will be posted on this page with a new date.
