Brewratio

Coffee brew ratio + timer + bean log. Local-only.

What it does

  • Live water-weight math from beans + ratio
  • Presets: V60, Chemex, Aeropress, French press, espresso, moka pot, cold brew
  • Timer with method-specific defaults
  • Bean library (roaster / origin / process / opened date)
  • Saved recipes with grind + water temperature
  • No account, no cloud upload

Privacy-first

Everything you create stays on your iPhone. No account. No cloud upload. No analytics SDK.
