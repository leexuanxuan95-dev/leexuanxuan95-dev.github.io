Petkit

A private timeline of everything you do for your pet. On-device.

What it does

  • Quick-log chips: fed, water, walk, play, medication, vet, grooming, weight, note
  • Sectioned timeline grouped by day
  • Weight chart with trend
  • Multi-pet with Pro; free tier includes one pet
  • No account, no cloud upload

Privacy-first

Everything you create stays on your iPhone. No account. No cloud upload. No analytics SDK.
